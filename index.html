About Our shop

Cindy's Cafe

Cindy's Cafe is a restaurant located at Barangay Tabi, Boac, Mrinduque. And can also be located at the Barangay Malusak, Boac, Marinduque branch. We offer Chicken Wing meal in your selected sauces, 7 inches Burger meal for 2, Delicious Milktea, Fruit Juices, Frappe and Shakes. We also offer all day breakfast and delicious snacks.
